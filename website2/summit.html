Annual Hisar Coding Summit

Visit the Website

The Coding Summit is an annual 2-day long event hosted in the Hisar High School, aiming to teach students of all ages to code. The event hosts over 300 participants each year and participants are able to choose from many workshops available. Although the workshops are only 6 hours long, attendees are capable of building their own applications at the end of the curriculum. The workshop instructors are high school students that are proficient in their language of instruction and are able to empathize with students that are beginners in programming.

- Simple Electronics with Arduino

- Intro to Programming with Python & Simple Electronics with Raspberry Pi

- iOS App Development with Swift

- Game Design and Development with Unity
