What is Game Jam?

It's a one-week long game hackaton. Participants are required to create a game according to the theme that is announced at the beginning of the week. After all games are submitted at the end of the week, our judges will review all games according to the criterias and pick the winners. Participation is limited to groups of 4, individuals are welcome.

We have prepared a series of Udemy videos to refresh your knowledge about game development or to help you from scratch. We will also have 3 Q&A sessions during the Gamejam week. You will be able to ask questions any time on the Discord channel created particularly for Gamejam.

Idea Development

All entertaining games start with a unique, creative idea. Learn how to develop games through practice and workshops in Hisar Jam.

Game Design

Game design is a very important aspect of game development. This is what makes your game attractive and popular. Learn game design by watching our workshops from last year.

Coding

Coding is a significant part of designing the game mechanics. Through coding, you can appeal to the user's senses with game mechanics or create an interesting storyline for your game.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the schedule?

The theme of the game jam will be announced in 00.00 on the 19th of May 2022. The submission portal will be closed at 23.59 on the 22nd of May 2022

How do I register?

You can register by filling out the form that appears when you click the "Register" button on the top of the page. Don't forget to join our Discord channel! Link provided at the end of registeration.

How are the winners chosen?

After revealing the theme, certain criteria will also be announced. Our judges will determine the most suitable winners in the light of these criteria.

Do I have to have prior knowledge?

No. We have released a video tutorial series for those who do not know how to create a game from scratch. You can build your knowledge by watching these videos. They will cover everything you need to know about game development for the Game Jam. Additionally, we will publish our documentation.
